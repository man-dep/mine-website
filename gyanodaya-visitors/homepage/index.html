Life at Gyanodaya School

Leadership Message

Prem Tamang

Principal

"Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers, It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you all to Gyanodaya English School. Education, in my view, is not only about gaining knowledge but also about shaping character, building confidence, and preparing for life’s challenges. At our school, we are deeply committed to nurturing young minds with discipline, values, and creativity. With the dedication of our experienced teachers, we strive to provide a learning environment where every child feels encouraged to explore, question, and discover. We believe that true education is a partnership between school, students, and parents. Therefore, your active involvement and continuous support play a vital role in shaping the future of our children. Our vision is to prepare students who are not only academically excellent but also socially responsible, morally strong, and globally competent. We aim to cultivate in them a sense of respect, responsibility, and resilience, so they can become confident individuals ready to face the future. As the Principal, I assure you that Gyanodaya English School will always remain a place where dreams are nurtured, talents are recognized, and every student is guided with care and dedication. Together, let us work to make education a journey of growth, wisdom, and success."

Sunil Dahal

Vice Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Guardians, It is my privilege to extend my heartfelt greetings to you all. At Gyanodaya English School, we believe that education is a continuous journey of growth and discovery. As the Vice Principal, my role is to support our Principal, teachers, and students in ensuring that this journey is meaningful and fruitful for everyone. We live in a rapidly changing world where knowledge, skills, and values are equally important. Our school strives to provide students not only with strong academic foundations but also with opportunities to develop confidence, creativity, discipline, and leadership qualities. With the guidance of our dedicated and experienced teachers, we are committed to helping each child reach their highest potential. I strongly believe that true education is built upon cooperation between school, students, and parents. Together, we can nurture a generation that is knowledgeable, responsible, and compassionate. We encourage our students to dream big, work hard, and stay rooted in values while preparing themselves for the challenges of tomorrow. Let us join hands in creating a learning environment where every student feels inspired, motivated, and empowered to achieve excellence in both academics and life."
